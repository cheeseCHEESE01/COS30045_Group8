About This Project

The data visualization of migration in India presents a multifaceted view of demographic movements within the nation.

This comprehensive visual representation encompasses a spectrum of crucial factors influencing migration trends. Alongside migration patterns, we gain insights into India's dynamic labor market with visuals depicting the unemployment rate and monthly wages in various countries. Furthermore, the visualization delves into India's vast population, offering a clear depiction of its size and distribution. Understanding the motivations behind Indian migration is pivotal, and the data visualization provides key insights into the factors driving this phenomenon. Additionally, the visualization highlights the correlation between disease prevalence and migration patterns, underscoring the importance of public health in demographic shifts. Lastly, the visualization captures the essence of India's global presence, showcasing immigration trends from India to various countries. These visuals collectively offer a comprehensive narrative of India's demographic landscape, serving as a valuable resource for policymakers, researchers, and stakeholders alike.
